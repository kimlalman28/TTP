I am a student at Queens College since Fall 2014. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Accounting. I was born in Trinidad and moved to NY at the age of 3. I am an only child. My favorite animal is the Panda. My interests consist of gaming, writing, sight-seeing, and gardening.
